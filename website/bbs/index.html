Our Story

Two adventurers,
one great run

Ankur & Akanksha
♥ ♥ ♥

As we approach a decade of being together, our journey feels both beautifully familiar and wonderfully new. What started with late office hangouts and shared ambitions soon turned into long evenings preparing for the GRE, dreaming about the future, and taking bold steps to move across the world.

Along the way, we've grown through every phase of life—building careers, creating a home, navigating the uncertainties of the pandemic, and finding joy in the little things. From thrilling adventures on snowy slopes to crazy hikes through scenic trails, each experience has brought us closer and shaped the life we cherish today.

And now, as we turn this next page, our hearts are fuller than ever. We are beyond excited to welcome the newest member of our family—our little bundle of joy—into a world already filled with so much love, laughter, and togetherness.
