# Assessment Framework

## Weekly assessment mix
Each week must include:
- vocabulary recognition
- grammar control
- reading comprehension
- listening comprehension
- writing output

## Balance
- 50-60% current week
- 25-30% recent review
- 15-20% older cumulative review

## Difficulty
- 40% easy recognition
- 35% medium controlled application
- 25% transfer or inference

## Reading tasks
Mix:
- direct factual questions
- sentence meaning questions
- detail finding
- simple inference

## Listening tasks
Mix:
- choose what you heard
- fill missing word
- ordering
- true/false
- match meaning
- short dictation

## Writing progression
Early:
- sentence completion
- word ordering
- transform one sentence
- answer guided questions

Middle:
- short paragraph
- short note
- message / email
- compare two routines / events

Later:
- opinion paragraph
- summary response
- practical email
- short explanation with connectors

## Writing rubric
Every writing test should score:
1. task completion
2. vocabulary range and appropriateness
3. grammar control
4. clarity and organization

## Feedback
Feedback should be specific, encouraging, and focused on 2-3 key improvements.
