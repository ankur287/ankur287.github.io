Week 1 Grammar

Main target: subject pronouns and etre basics. Review target: foundation week: pronunciation, classroom routines, and memory habits.

Main Grammar Target

introduce yourself, recognize classroom people and objects, and write a short controlled profile.

PatternUseExample
je suisI am for identity or locationJe suis Alex.
tu esyou are for a familiar personTu es Samira.
il est / elle esthe is / she isIl est Marc. Elle est Madame Dupont.
nous sommeswe areNous sommes en classe.
vous etesyou are, formal or pluralVous etes Madame Dupont.
ils/elles sontthey areIls sont en classe.

Review Grammar Target

This first week builds the memory base that later weeks will recycle inside examples, readings, listenings, writing prompts, and quizzes.

foundation week: pronunciation, classroom routines, and memory habits
