Week 1 Listening

The listening stays very close to the reading level. Listen for names, greetings, and forms of être.

1. Listening warm-up

Before you listen, say these aloud:

  • Bonjour
  • Je m'appelle Alex
  • Elle est Samira
  • Nous sommes en classe lundi

2. Main listening dialogue

Audio player
Reveal transcript after listening

Bonjour. Je m'appelle Alex. Tu es Samira ? Oui, je suis Samira. Il est Marc. Madame Dupont est la professeure. Nous sommes en classe lundi. Merci. Au revoir.

4. First listen task

  1. Which names do you hear?
  2. Do you hear bonjour or bonsoir?
  3. Which day do you hear?
Answer reveal
  1. Alex, Samira, Marc, Madame Dupont
  2. bonjour
  3. lundi

5. Second listen task

  1. True or false: Alex says his name.
  2. True or false: The dialogue says “Nous sommes en classe mardi.”
  3. Match the meaning: Merci = ?
Answer reveal
  1. True.
  2. False. The day is lundi.
  3. Thank you.

6. Dictation / gap-fill

Listen again and fill the missing word:

  1. Je m'appelle ______.
  2. Oui, je suis ______.
  3. Nous sommes en classe ______.
Reveal answers
  1. Alex
  2. Samira
  3. lundi
