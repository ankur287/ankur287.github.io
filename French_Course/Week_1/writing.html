Week 1 Writing

You are not expected to write freely yet. Start with guided sentences, then move to a supported self-introduction.

1. Sentence warm-up

  1. Complete: Bonjour, je m'________ Alex.
  2. Complete: Je ________ Alex.
  3. Order the words: Samira / est / elle
  4. Order the words: sommes / nous / en classe
Reveal answers
  1. appelle
  2. suis
  3. Elle est Samira.
  4. Nous sommes en classe.

2. Guided writing

Use the frame and change only the highlighted part.

  • Bonjour, je m'appelle _____.
  • Je suis _____.
  • Nous sommes en classe _____.
  • Merci, _____.

3. Controlled writing

Write 3 short sentences:

  1. Your greeting
  2. Your name sentence
  3. One sentence with a day
Possible model

Bonjour. Je m'appelle Alex. Nous sommes en classe lundi.

4. Writing test

Task: Write a 4–6 sentence self-introduction. Use at least:

  • one greeting
  • your name
  • two sentences with être
  • one day of the week
  • one courtesy word

Support bank: bonjour, salut, je m'appelle, je suis, nous sommes, lundi, mardi, merci, au revoir

5. Checklist before reveal / submit

  • Did you start with a greeting?
  • Did you write your name?
  • Did you use the correct form of être?
  • Did you include one day?
  • Did you finish clearly?

6. Model answer

Show model answer

Bonjour. Je m'appelle Alex. Je suis en classe. Samira est en classe aussi. Nous sommes en classe lundi. Merci, Madame Dupont. Au revoir.

Writing rubric

Task completionIncludes 4–6 sentences and all requested parts.
Vocabulary useUses Week 1 words accurately and appropriately.
Grammar controlUses subject pronouns and forms of être correctly most of the time.
ClarityThe writing is easy to follow and organized in a simple order.
