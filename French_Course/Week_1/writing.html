Week 1 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Bonjour, je m'appelle Alex.
  2. Change the person or object: Salut, Samira !
  3. Add one review word: Bonjour, je m'appelle Alex.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Bonjour, je m'appelle Alex. ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: bonjour, salut, au revoir, merci, s'il vous plaît, je. Add two review chunks: bonjour, salut.

Writing Test

Write 5-7 short sentences introducing yourself. Use je m appelle, je suis, one person, one place, one day, and one classroom word.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 1's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
