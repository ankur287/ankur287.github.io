Week 1 — Bonjour, je suis Alex

This week starts the course gently. You will learn how to greet people, say your name, use basic subject pronouns, and make simple sentences with être.

Week 1 New grammar: subject pronouns + être Review: none yet, but we recycle all Week 1 language often

What is new

  • Greetings: bonjour, salut, au revoir
  • Courtesy words: merci, s'il vous plaît
  • Names and introductions: je m'appelle…, je suis…
  • Days of the week: lundi to dimanche
  • Main grammar: je / tu / il / elle / nous / vous / ils / elles + être

What is being reviewed

Because this is Week 1, there is no earlier grammar to review. Instead, the course builds memory through repeated use of the same small set of words across vocabulary, grammar, reading, listening, writing, and quiz pages.

Learning goals

  • Read and understand a very short introduction between recurring course characters.
  • Hear and recognize familiar words in a short audio dialogue.
  • Write a 4–6 sentence self-introduction with support.

Suggested study order

  1. Vocabulary
  2. Grammar
  3. Reading
  4. Listening
  5. Writing
  6. Quiz
