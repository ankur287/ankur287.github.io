Week 1 — Bonjour, je suis Alex

This week builds greetings, introductions, être, classroom words, learning-task words, days as one group, and months as one group.

75 vocabulary itemssubject pronouns + êtresentence-based practice

What is new

  • Greetings, courtesy, classroom people, and classroom objects
  • Subject pronouns and core être forms
  • Learning words: reading, listening, writing, question, answer
  • Grouped calendar sets: days and months

Learning goals

  • Understand a class introduction with real classroom words.
  • Hear and identify names, objects, and être forms.
  • Write a 5-7 sentence self-introduction with a classroom detail.
