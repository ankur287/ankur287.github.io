Week 1 Vocabulary

Small, useful vocabulary only. These words reappear in the reading, listening, writing, and quiz pages.

1. New this week

bonjour

Pronunciation: bohn-ZHOOR

English: hello / good day

Example: Bonjour, je m'appelle Alex.

Example in English: Hello, my name is Alex.

newreadinglisteningwriting

salut

Pronunciation: sah-LUU

English: hi / bye (informal)

Example: Salut, Samira !

Example in English: Hi, Samira!

newreadinglistening

au revoir

Pronunciation: oh ruh-VWAHR

English: goodbye

Example: Au revoir, Madame Dupont.

Example in English: Goodbye, Madame Dupont.

newlistening

merci

Pronunciation: mehr-SEE

English: thank you

Example: Merci, Marc.

Example in English: Thank you, Marc.
