Week 1 Reading

Short and controlled. The reading uses the same names and phrases you saw in vocabulary and grammar.

1. Warm-up review

  • bonjour = hello
  • je m'appelle = my name is
  • je suis = I am
  • elle est = she is

2. Main reading passage

Audio player

Bonjour ! Je m'appelle Alex. Je suis en classe. Elle est Samira. Il est Marc. Madame Dupont est la professeure. Nous sommes en classe lundi. Merci, Madame Dupont. Au revoir !

4. Sentence-by-sentence breakdown

  1. Bonjour !
    Hello!
  2. Je m'appelle Alex.
    My name is Alex.
  3. Je suis en classe.
    I am in class.
  4. Elle est Samira.
    She is Samira.
  5. Il est Marc.
    He is Marc.
  6. Madame Dupont est la professeure.
    Madame Dupont is the teacher.
  7. Nous sommes en classe lundi.
    We are in class on Monday.
  8. Merci, Madame Dupont. Au revoir !
    Thank you, Madame Dupont. Goodbye!

5. Hidden English translation

Show full translation

Hello! My name is Alex. I am in class. She is Samira. He is Marc. Madame Dupont is the teacher. We are in class on Monday. Thank you, Madame Dupont. Goodbye!

6. Comprehension questions

  1. What is the learner’s name?
  2. Who is Samira?
  3. Who is Marc?
  4. Who is the teacher?
  5. What day are they in class?
Reveal answers
  1. Alex.
  2. She is a classmate / friend in the text.
  3. He is Marc.
  4. Madame Dupont.
  5. Monday.
