3. Sentence-by-sentence breakdown

  1. Je suis étudiant.
    English

    I am a student.

  2. Nous sommes en classe lundi avec Madame Dupont.
    English

    We are in class on Monday with Ms. Dupont.

  3. Le tableau, la table, la chaise, le livre, le cahier et le stylo sont en classe.
    English

    The board, table, chair, book, notebook, and pen are in class.

  4. La phrase est courte. La question est facile.
    English

    The sentence is short. The question is easy.

4. Hidden English translation

Show full translation

Hello. My name is Alex. I am a student. Samira is a friend. Marc is here too. We are in class on Monday with Ms. Dupont. The room is simple. The board, table, chair, book, notebook, and pen are in class. The sentence is short. The question is easy. Thank you, Ms. Dupont. Goodbye.
