Week 14 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Il faut boire de l'eau.
  2. Change the person or object: Je dois rester a la maison.
  3. Add one review word: Hier, j'ai parle avec Samira.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Il faut boire de ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: il faut, je dois, vous devez, j'ai besoin de, j'ai mal a la tete, j'ai mal au ventre. Add two review chunks: j'ai parle, avant.

Writing Test

Write a short health note of 8-10 sentences. Say where you have pain, what happened yesterday, what the weather was like, what you must do now, and one polite message to Madame Dupont or the clinic.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 14's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
