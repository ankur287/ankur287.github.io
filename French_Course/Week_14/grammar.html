Week 14 Grammar

Main target: faut, devoir, and avoir mal a for health needs. Review target: past narration with passe compose and imperfect background.

Main Grammar Target

describe a simple health problem, say what is necessary, and write a short note using familiar past narration as support.

PatternUseExample
il faut + infinitiveit is necessary to do somethingIl faut boire de l eau.
je dois + infinitiveI must / I have toJe dois rester a la maison.
vous devez + infinitiveyou must / you have toVous devez appeler la clinique.
j'ai mal a la / au / auxI have pain in a body partJ'ai mal a la tete.
avoir besoin deto need somethingJ'ai besoin de repos.
past narration + health actionwhat happened and what is needed nowHier, j'ai eu mal au ventre, alors je dois me reposer.

Review Grammar Target

Older grammar is not separate homework here. It appears inside the new examples, the reading, the listening, the writing prompts, and the quiz.

past narration with passe compose and imperfect background

Pattern Ladder

  1. Il faut boire de l'eau.
  2. Je dois rester a la maison.
  3. Vous devez appeler la clinique.
  4. J'ai besoin de repos.
  5. Aujourd'hui, j'ai mal a la tete.
  6. Hier, j'ai eu mal au ventre.

Watch out: Use a la before feminine body parts, au before masculine body parts, and aux before plural body parts: a la tete, au dos, aux yeux. Keep advice chunks short: il faut boire, je dois appeler.
