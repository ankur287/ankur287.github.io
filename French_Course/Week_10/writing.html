Week 10 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Aujourd'hui, il pleut.
  2. Change the person or object: En hiver, il neige parfois.
  3. Add one older review word: Je suis en classe avec Samira.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Aujourd'hui, il pleut. ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: il pleut, il neige, il fait froid, il fait chaud, il fait beau, il y a du vent. Add two review chunks: je suis, j'ai.

Writing Test

Write 9 sentences about this week. Include three weather expressions, two time markers, one routine sentence, and one plan depending on weather.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 10's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
