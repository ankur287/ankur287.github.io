Week 26 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: La nouvelle est courte.
  2. Change the person or object: Un avis public est affiche.
  3. Add one review word: Ce message est formel.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. La nouvelle est courte. ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: une nouvelle, un avis public, selon l avis, le quartier, le service, un changement. Add two review chunks: formel / formelle, lis la question.

Writing Test

Write a short news summary of 7-9 sentences. Include the main event, where it happens, one reason, two details, and one personal next step.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 26's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
