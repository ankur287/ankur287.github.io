Week 24 Grammar

Main target: formal vs informal register. Review target: politeness and conditional.

Main Grammar Target

compare informal and formal messages and write a short formal email with polite chunks.

PatternUseExample

je vous ecris pourI am writing toJe vous ecris pour confirmer mon rendez-vous.
conditional politenesspolite requestPourriez-vous confirmer l heure ?
