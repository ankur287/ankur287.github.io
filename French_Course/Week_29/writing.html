Week 29 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Madame Dupont organise un mock CLB.
  2. Change the person or object: Je fais un test blanc.
  3. Add one review word: Le rapport est court.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Madame Dupont organise un ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: un mock CLB, un test blanc, pendant vingt minutes, le temps limite, gerer le temps, faire un plan. Add two review chunks: un rapport, deduire.

Writing Test

Timed mock writing: in 20 minutes, write 7-9 sentences. Summarize a notice, give two evidence details, and write your next step or opinion.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 29's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
