Week 15 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Je pense que la lecon est utile.
  2. Change the person or object: J'aime le cafe calme.
  3. Add one review word: Avant, j'habitais pres du parc.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Je pense que la ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: je pense que, j'aime, je n'aime pas, je prefere, parce que, a mon avis. Add two review chunks: avant, il faut.

Writing Test

Write an opinion paragraph of 8-10 short sentences. Say what you like, what you prefer, one thing you do not like, and why. Include one health sentence and one past narration sentence.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 15's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
