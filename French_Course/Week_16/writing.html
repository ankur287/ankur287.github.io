Week 16 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Je vais appeler la clinique.
  2. Change the person or object: Tu vas prendre le bus.
  3. Add one review word: Il faut boire de l'eau.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Je vais appeler la ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: je vais, tu vas, il va / elle va, nous allons, demain, la semaine prochaine. Add two review chunks: il faut, je pense que.

Writing Test

Write 8-10 sentences about next week. Use at least five futur proche forms, three time markers, one opinion, and one health or appointment sentence.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 16's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
