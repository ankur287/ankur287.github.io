Week 12 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Hier, j'ai parle avec Samira.
  2. Change the person or object: Tu as parle avec Madame Dupont.
  3. Add one older review word: Je suis en classe avec Samira.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Hier, j'ai parle avec ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: j'ai parle, tu as parle, il a parle / elle a parle, nous avons parle, j'ai travaille, j'ai etudie. Add two review chunks: je suis, j'ai.

Writing Test

Write a 10-12 sentence diary about yesterday. Use hier, at least six j ai chunks, one food item, one place, one family or friend detail, and one today/yesterday contrast.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 12's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
