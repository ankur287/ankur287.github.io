Week 4 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: J'ai une petite cle dans mon sac.
  2. Change the person or object: Le bureau est grand.
  3. Add one older review word: Je suis en classe avec Samira.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. J'ai une petite cle ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: petit / petite, grand / grande, rouge, bleu / bleue, vert / verte, jaune. Add two review chunks: je suis, j'ai.

Writing Test

Write 7 sentences describing one person and one room. Use five adjectives and at least three articles.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 4's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
