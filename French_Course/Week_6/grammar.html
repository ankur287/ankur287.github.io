Week 6 Grammar

Main target: partitives and polite requests. Review target: possessives and articles in cafe sentences.

Main Grammar Target

order food politely and describe food preferences with du, de la, de l, des, and pas de.

PatternUseExample
dusome before masculine singulardu pain
de lasome before feminine singularde la soupe
de l'some before vowel soundde l'eau
dessome pluraldes pommes
pas denot any after negationJe ne veux pas de cafe.

Review Grammar Target

Older grammar is not separate homework here. It appears inside the new examples, the reading, the listening, the writing prompts, and the quiz.

possessives and articles in cafe sentences

Pattern Ladder

  1. Je voudrais du cafe, s il vous plait.
  2. Alex prend du pain.
  3. Samira prend de la soupe.
  4. Je voudrais de l'eau.
  5. Marc ne veut pas de dessert.
  6. Je voudrais un cafe chaud.

Compare And Notice

Je voudrais du cafe, s il vous plait.

English and why

I would like some coffee, please.

This sentence combines Week 6's target with older review language.

English and why

Je voudrais un cafe chaud.

English and why

I would like a hot coffee.

This sentence combines Week 6's target with older review language.

Watch out: After a negative, use pas de: Je voudrais du cafe, but Je ne veux pas de cafe.
