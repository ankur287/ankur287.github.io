Week 23 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Alex est fatigue; cependant, il continue.
  2. Change the person or object: Il pleut, donc Alex prend le bus.
  3. Add one review word: Il faut que je revise.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Alex est fatigue; cependant, ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: cependant, donc, pourtant, pendant que, grace a, a cause de. Add two review chunks: il faut que, est ecrit / ecrite.

Writing Test

Write a linked paragraph of 8-10 sentences. Use at least six connectors, one relative clause with qui or que, and one review sentence with a pronoun.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 23's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
