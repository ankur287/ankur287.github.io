Week 30 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: La revision finale commence aujourd hui.
  2. Change the person or object: Je peux continuer apres la semaine trente.
  3. Add one review word: Je peux deduire la reponse.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. La revision finale commence ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: revision finale, je peux continuer, j ai progresse, je comprends mieux, je peux resumer, je peux repondre. Add two review chunks: deduire, un mock CLB.

Writing Test

Final mixed writing: write 10-12 sentences. Summarize a message, give evidence, state an opinion, make a polite request, and describe your next study plan.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 30's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
