Week 25 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Lis la question avant le texte.
  2. Change the person or object: Ecoute deux fois avant de repondre.
  3. Add one review word: Alex est fatigue; cependant, il continue.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Lis la question avant ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: lis la question, ecoute deux fois, choisis la bonne reponse, coche vrai ou faux, complete la phrase, associe les mots. Add two review chunks: cependant, formel / formelle.

Writing Test

Write a short summary and response. Include 3 sentences summarizing a message or notice, 2 evidence sentences, and 2 opinion or next-step sentences.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 25's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
