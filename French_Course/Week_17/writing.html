Week 17 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Je voudrais prendre rendez-vous.
  2. Change the person or object: Pourriez-vous repeter ?
  3. Add one review word: Je pense que la lecon est utile.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Je voudrais prendre rendez-vous. ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: je voudrais, pourriez-vous, est-ce que je pourrais, j'aimerais, s il vous plait, merci beaucoup. Add two review chunks: je pense que, je vais.

Writing Test

Write a polite request message of 8-10 sentences or lines. Ask for an appointment or help, give one reason, include one future plan, and use at least three polite chunks.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 17's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
