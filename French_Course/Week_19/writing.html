Week 19 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: La note qui aide Alex est courte.
  2. Change the person or object: La note que je lis est claire.
  3. Add one review word: Je voudrais prendre rendez-vous.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. La note qui aide ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: qui, que, dont, qui est, que je lis, que je prends. Add two review chunks: je voudrais, le.

Writing Test

Write 8-10 sentences describing a person, place, object, and appointment. Use at least three sentences with qui, two with que, and one recognition sentence with dont.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 19's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
