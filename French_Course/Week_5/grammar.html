Week 5 Grammar

Main target: possessive adjectives. Review target: adjective agreement with family and objects.

Main Grammar Target

describe family members and belongings using mon, ma, mes, son, sa, ses, notre, and nos.

PatternUseExample

  3. Mes cahiers sont dans mon sac.
  4. Ton sac est noir.
  5. Ta chambre est claire.
