# Quality Assurance Checklist

## Continuity
- Does this week clearly connect to previous weeks?
- Does it state what is being reviewed?
- Does it reuse the story world where useful?
- Can the learner revise previous-week content while studying the current week without going back?
- Is previous-week vocabulary and grammar embedded in the new reading, listening, writing, examples, and quiz?

## Vocabulary
- Is the new load reasonable?
- Are words high-frequency and useful?
- Does each important word reappear multiple times?
- Are there too many random one-off words?
- Does the vocabulary page include a random French → English and English → French drill?

## Grammar
- Is there one main grammar focus?
- Is one older grammar focus reviewed?
- Are examples mostly built from known words?
- Is the explanation simple and accurate?

## Reading
- Is the passage mostly comprehensible?
- Are new words limited and supported?
- Are questions level-appropriate?

## Listening
- Is the listening based on learned material?
- Is it close to the reading level?
- Is there cumulative review?

## Writing
- Does writing move from guided to freer output?
- Is there a real writing test?
- Is there a rubric?
- Is the model answer hidden by default?

## Website
- Does the output include root index.html?
- Does the week folder include all required files?
- Are links relative?
- Is mobile use comfortable?
- Does the quiz contain 50 questions?

## Final question
- Would the learner remember more after this week, or only see more content?
