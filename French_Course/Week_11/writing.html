Week 11 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Je travaille au bureau.
  2. Change the person or object: Tu travailles avec Marc.
  3. Add one older review word: Je suis en classe avec Samira.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Je travaille au bureau. ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: je travaille, tu travailles, il travaille / elle travaille, nous travaillons, j'etudie, je prepare. Add two review chunks: je suis, j'ai.

Writing Test

Write 10 sentences about a work or study day. Use five present-tense verbs, two time markers, one weather expression, and one routine sentence.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 11's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
