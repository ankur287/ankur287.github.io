Week 13 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Avant, j'habitais pres du parc.
  2. Change the person or object: Quand j'etais enfant, je jouais au parc.
  3. Add one review word: Je travaille au bureau.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Avant, j'habitais pres du ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: avant, quand, enfant, souvent, toujours, parfois. Add two review chunks: je travaille, j'ai parle.

Writing Test

Write 10-12 short sentences comparing before and yesterday. Use at least four imperfect chunks, three passe compose chunks, two older review places or foods, and one weather sentence.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 13's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
