# Curriculum Master Plan — 30 Weeks

## Phase 1 — Foundations
### Week 1
- Main grammar: subject pronouns + être basics
- Review grammar: none
- New vocabulary: greetings, names, courtesy words, days
- Reading/listening outcome: simple introductions
- Writing outcome: self-introduction in 4-6 sentences

### Week 2
- Main grammar: avoir basics + age
- Review grammar: être
- New vocabulary: age, family basics, numbers, common objects
- Reading/listening outcome: introducing self and family
- Writing outcome: short personal profile

### Week 3
- Main grammar: definite/indefinite articles + gender awareness
- Review grammar: être / avoir
- New vocabulary: classroom and household nouns
- Reading/listening outcome: describing what someone has
- Writing outcome: describe items in a room

### Week 4
- Main grammar: adjective agreement basics
- Review grammar: articles and gender
- New vocabulary: colors, size, common adjectives
- Reading/listening outcome: simple descriptions
- Writing outcome: describe a person and a room

## Phase 2 — Daily life
### Week 5
- Main grammar: possessive adjectives
- Review grammar: adjective agreement
- New vocabulary: family members and relationships
- Reading/listening outcome: talking about family
- Writing outcome: describe your family

### Week 6
- Main grammar: partitive basics + polite requests
- Review grammar: possessives / articles
- New vocabulary: food, drinks, restaurant phrases
- Reading/listening outcome: cafe or restaurant dialogue
- Writing outcome: order a meal / write food preferences

### Week 7
- Main grammar: question forms + basic vouloir / prendre
- Review grammar: numbers and articles
- New vocabulary: shops, clothes, prices, quantities
- Reading/listening outcome: shopping dialogue
- Writing outcome: short shopping conversation or list

### Week 8
- Main grammar: il y a + prepositions of place
- Review grammar: question forms
- New vocabulary: transport, places in town, direction words
- Reading/listening outcome: asking for and giving directions
- Writing outcome: explain a simple route

## Phase 3 — Routine and time
### Week 9
- Main grammar: reflexive verbs basics
- Review grammar: place expressions
- New vocabulary: rooms, furniture, daily activities
- Reading/listening outcome: daily routine
- Writing outcome: my typical day

### Week 10
- Main grammar: weather expressions + time markers
- Review grammar: reflexive verbs
- New vocabulary: weather, seasons, dates, time expressions
- Reading/listening outcome: plans depending on weather
- Writing outcome: describe a week / forecast / routine

### Week 11
- Main grammar: regular present tense consolidation
- Review grammar: time expressions
- New vocabulary: jobs, workplace, study tasks
- Reading/listening outcome: workday or school day
- Writing outcome: describe your job or study day

### Week 12
- Main grammar: passé composé introduction
- Review grammar: present tense anchors
- New vocabulary: yesterday, travel, errands, completed actions
- Reading/listening outcome: what someone did yesterday
- Writing outcome: past-tense diary

## Phase 4 — Story building
### Week 13
- Main grammar: imperfect introduction and contrast with passé composé
- Review grammar: passé composé
- New vocabulary: childhood, habits, scene-setting words
- Reading/listening outcome: story with background + event
- Writing outcome: compare before and yesterday

### Week 14
- Main grammar: faut / devoir / avoir mal à
- Review grammar: past narration
- New vocabulary: body, symptoms, health actions
- Reading/listening outcome: clinic dialogue
- Writing outcome: describe symptoms / write a health note

### Week 15
- Main grammar: opinions and preferences
- Review grammar: adjective agreement
- New vocabulary: feelings, opinions, likes/dislikes
- Reading/listening outcome: discussing preferences
- Writing outcome: opinion paragraph

### Week 16
- Main grammar: futur proche, then simple future if appropriate
- Review grammar: time expressions and opinions
- New vocabulary: plans, goals, travel, appointments
- Reading/listening outcome: future plans
- Writing outcome: next week plans

## Phase 5 — Expanding control
### Week 17
- Main grammar: conditional for politeness
- Review grammar: future forms
- New vocabulary: requests, services, appointments
- Reading/listening outcome: polite booking / asking
- Writing outcome: polite request message

### Week 18
- Main grammar: direct and indirect pronouns, staged
- Review grammar: present/past anchors
- New vocabulary: high-frequency support verbs
- Reading/listening outcome: short dialogue chains
- Writing outcome: rewrite with pronouns

### Week 19
- Main grammar: qui, que, dont basics
- Review grammar: adjective and noun linking
- New vocabulary: descriptive detail and possession
- Reading/listening outcome: describing in more detail
- Writing outcome: describe person/place/object with relative clauses

### Week 20
- Main grammar: Canadian French recognition focus
- Review grammar: relative clauses and pronouns
- New vocabulary: high-value Quebec / Canadian French differences
- Reading/listening outcome: standard vs Canadian usage
- Writing outcome: compare expressions

## Phase 6 — Higher-level comprehension
### Week 21
- Main grammar: subjunctive awareness through common chunks
- Review grammar: opinions and necessity
- New vocabulary: necessity, emotion, doubt
- Reading/listening outcome: recommendation / opinion text
- Writing outcome: sentence-level guided production

### Week 22
- Main grammar: passive voice basics
- Review grammar: past participles and agreement awareness
- New vocabulary: news/process language
- Reading/listening outcome: informational texts
- Writing outcome: active/passive transformations

### Week 23
- Main grammar: complex connectors
- Review grammar: relative clauses
- New vocabulary: discourse markers
- Reading/listening outcome: longer multi-clause text
- Writing outcome: linked paragraph

### Week 24
- Main grammar: formal vs informal register
- Review grammar: politeness and conditional
- New vocabulary: formal email and message language
- Reading/listening outcome: compare informal text and formal email
- Writing outcome: formal email

## Phase 7 — CLB-oriented training
### Week 25
- Main grammar: no major new grammar
- Review grammar: targeted weak points
- New vocabulary: instruction words and question stems
- Reading/listening outcome: strategy-based tasks
- Writing outcome: summary and response

### Week 26
- Main grammar: news style review
- Review grammar: passive, past, connectors
- New vocabulary: current events, public topics
- Reading/listening outcome: simplified news
- Writing outcome: short news summary

### Week 27
- Main grammar: formal complex sentence control
- Review grammar: formal register
- New vocabulary: workplace, study, analysis verbs
- Reading/listening outcome: short report or notice
- Writing outcome: professional message / short summary

### Week 28
- Main grammar: inference and discourse tracking
- Review grammar: connectors and pronouns
- New vocabulary: inference signals
- Reading/listening outcome: longer passage
- Writing outcome: paragraph response with evidence

### Week 29
- Main grammar: review only
- Review grammar: personalized weak points
- New vocabulary: minimal
- Reading/listening outcome: mock CLB-style tasks
- Writing outcome: timed mock writing

### Week 30
- Main grammar: review only
- Review grammar: personalized weak points
- New vocabulary: only if needed
- Reading/listening outcome: final mixed review
- Writing outcome: final mixed review
