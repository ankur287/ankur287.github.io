Week 22 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: La note est ecrite par Samira.
  2. Change the person or object: Le courriel est envoye par Alex.
  3. Add one review word: Nous reconnaissons le francais canadien.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. La note est ecrite ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: est ecrit / ecrite, est envoye / envoyee, est donne / donnee, est rempli / remplie, est explique / expliquee, est affiche / affichee. Add two review chunks: le francais canadien, il faut que.

Writing Test

Transform 6 active sentences into passive sentences, then write 3 short process sentences about a clinic, class, or message.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 22's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
