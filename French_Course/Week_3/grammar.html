Week 3 Grammar

Main target: definite and indefinite articles with gender awareness. Review target: etre and avoir in descriptions of things people have.

Main Grammar Target

describe what Alex, Samira, and Marc have in a room using un, une, le, la, les, and des.

PatternUseExample
una/an before a masculine singular nounun livre
unea/an before a feminine singular nounune lampe
le / lathe before masculine / feminine singular nounsle bureau / la table
l'the before a vowel soundl'ordinateur
les / desthe plural / some pluralles livres / des cahiers

Review Grammar Target

Older grammar is not separate homework here. It appears inside the new examples, the reading, the listening, the writing prompts, and the quiz.

etre and avoir in descriptions of things people have

Pattern Ladder

  1. J'ai un livre dans mon sac.
  2. Samira a une lampe sur la table.
  3. Le livre est sur le bureau.
  4. La lampe est pres du lit.
  5. L'ordinateur est dans la classe.
  6. Les stylos sont sur la table.

Compare And Notice

J'ai un livre dans mon sac.

English and why

I have a book in my bag.

This sentence combines Week 3's target with older review language.

Watch out: Learn each noun with its article. English does not show noun gender, so memorize un livre and une lampe as whole chunks.
