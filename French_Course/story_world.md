# Story World

Use these recurring people, places, and situations so the course feels connected.

## People
- Alex: main learner character
- Samira: friend, classmate, or coworker
- Marc: neighbor or friend
- Madame Dupont: teacher, receptionist, or service worker
- The Martin family: useful for family and home themes

## Places
- apartment
- café
- grocery store
- bus stop / metro station
- language class
- office / school
- clinic
- neighborhood park

## Situations
- introducing yourself
- talking about family
- ordering food
- shopping
- asking directions
- describing routine
- talking about yesterday
- making plans
- sending messages
- asking for help
- describing health issues

## Continuity rule
Reuse the same people, neighborhood, and places when possible.
