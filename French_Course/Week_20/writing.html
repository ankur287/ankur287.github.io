Week 20 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Nous reconnaissons le francais canadien.
  2. Change the person or object: Au Quebec, on entend parfois des mots differents.
  3. Add one review word: Je prends le bus. Je le prends.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Nous reconnaissons le francais ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: le francais canadien, au Quebec, une region, standard, une expression, reconnaitre. Add two review chunks: le, qui.

Writing Test

Write 8-10 comparison sentences. Compare at least five Canadian French recognition words with standard or English meanings. Include one qui/que sentence and one pronoun review sentence.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 20's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
