Week 2 Vocabulary

This is a 75-item ordered bank. Number, day, and month sequences are grouped into one card each so you learn useful sets without wasting vocabulary slots.

1. Ordered vocabulary bank: 75 items

Read the example first and guess the meaning before revealing English.

Pronunciation: zay-ROH, uhn, duh...

Group: zéro, un, deux, trois, quatre, cinq, six, sept, huit, neuf, dix

Meaning: zéro, un, deux, trois, quatre, cinq, six, sept, huit, neuf, dix
