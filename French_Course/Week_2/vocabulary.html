Week 2 Vocabulary

First review Week 1. Then learn the small new set that you need for age, family, and personal profiles.

1. New this week

j'ai new grammar appears in reading

Pronunciation: zhay

Example: J'ai un livre.

English: I have

Sentence English: I have a book.

tu as / il a / elle a / nous avons / vous avez new grammar

Pronunciation: too ah / eel ah / el ah / noo zah-VOHN / voo zah-VAY

Example: Elle a un téléphone.

English: you have / he has / she has / we have / you have

Sentence English: She has a phone.

ans new age

Pronunciation: ahn

Example: J'ai 30 ans.

English: years old

Sentence English: I am 30 years old.

la famille new appears in writing

Pronunciation: lah fah-MEE

Example: J'ai une famille.

English: family

Sentence English: I have a family.

la mère / le père new

Pronunciation: mehr / pehr

Example: Marc a une mère et un père.

English: mother / father

Sentence English: Marc has a mother and a father.

le frère / la sœur new

Pronunciation: frair / suhr

Example: Samira a un frère.

English: brother / sister

Sentence English: Samira has a brother.

un livre / un téléphone new common objects

Pronunciation: lEE-vruh / tay-lay-FOHN

Example: J'ai un téléphone.

English: a book / a phone

Sentence English: I have a phone.

Numbers 0-10 new age support

zéro, un, deux, trois, quatre, cinq, six, sept, huit, neuf, dix

Pronunciation: zay-ROH, uhn, duh, trwah, kaht-ruh, sank, sees, set, weet, nuhf, dees

Example: Marc a dix ans.

English: zero to ten

Sentence English: Marc is ten years old.

2. Review words from recent weeks

bonjour / merci / au revoir review

Example: Bonjour, Madame Dupont. Merci. Au revoir.

Hello, Ms. Dupont. Thank you. Goodbye.

je m'appelle / je suis review

Example: Je m'appelle Alex. Je suis Alex.

My name is Alex. I am Alex.

3. Older core words returning this week

These Week 1 days return inside reading, listening, and quiz work: lundi, mardi, mercredi, jeudi, vendredi, samedi, dimanche.

4. Built with words you already know

Known pattern from Week 1: Je suis Alex.

New pattern from Week 2: J'ai un téléphone.

Combined profile: Bonjour. Je m'appelle Alex. Je suis Alex. J'ai 30 ans. J'ai un frère.

5. Mini practice

A. Which line means “I have a book”?

1. Je suis un livre.
2. J'ai un livre.
3. Je m'appelle livre.

Answer

2. J'ai un livre.

B. Fill the gap: Samira ___ un téléphone.

Answer

a → Samira a un téléphone.
