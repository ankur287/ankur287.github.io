Week 2 Quiz

This quiz mixes current week material, recent review, and a small cumulative check.

1. Quick review quiz

1. What means “goodbye”?



Correct: au revoir is goodbye.

2. Choose the correct form: Je ___ Alex.



Correct: Je suis Alex.

2. Main weekly quiz

3. What means “I have”?



Correct: j'ai.

4. Which sentence says “I am 30 years old”?



Correct: J'ai 30 ans.

5. Fill the idea: Samira ___ un téléphone.



Correct: Samira a un téléphone.

6. Which word means brother?



Correct: frère.

3. Reading / listening mini checks

7. In this week’s reading, who has the book?



Correct: Madame Dupont has the book.

8. In the listening, which day do you hear?



Correct: lundi.

4. Cumulative review block

9. Which line is a correct self-introduction with Week 1 + Week 2 material?



Correct: Bonjour. Je m'appelle Alex. J'ai 30 ans.

10. Which word is a day of the week?



Correct: mardi.
