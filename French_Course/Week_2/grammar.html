Week 2 Grammar

Main target: avoir basics and age. Review target: etre, greetings, classroom words, days, and months from Week 1.

Main Grammar Target

write a short personal profile with identity, age, family/object details, and one negative sentence.

PatternUseExample
j'aiI have / I am for ageJ'ai dix ans.
tu asyou haveTu as un telephone.
il/elle ahe/she hasElle a une soeur.
nous avonswe haveNous avons un livre.
je n ai pas deI do not have anyJe n'ai pas de telephone.
etre + avoir contrastidentity vs possessionJe suis Alex. J'ai un cahier.
