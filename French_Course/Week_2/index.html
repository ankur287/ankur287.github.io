Week 2 — J'ai dix ans

New this week: avoir basics, saying age, family basics, numbers 0-10, and a few common objects.

Review this week: greetings, names, être basics, and day names from Week 1.

Learning goals: understand and write a short personal profile, introduce family members, and say what someone has.

Suggested study order: Vocabulary → Grammar → Reading → Listening → Writing → Quiz

Why this week fits Week 1

Last week you learned how to say who someone is: je suis Alex. This week you add what someone has and how old someone is: j'ai un livre, j'ai dix ans. The same characters return: Alex, Samira, Marc, and Madame Dupont.
