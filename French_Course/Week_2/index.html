Week 2 — J'ai dix ans

New this week: 75 ordered vocabulary items, avoir basics, age, family/object words, personal information, simple questions, and negation with je n'ai pas.

Review this week: Week 1 greetings, être, classroom words, days, months, and learning words are used inside examples and tests.

Learning goals: understand and write a short profile with identity, age, objects, and one negative sentence.

Suggested study order: Vocabulary → Grammar → Reading → Listening → Writing → Quiz

Why this week fits Week 1

Week 1 said who someone is: je suis étudiante. Week 2 adds what someone has: j'ai un sac, Samira a une question, and nous avons la même page.
