Week 2 Reading

This reading is mostly built from Week 1 language plus the new Week 2 avoir and age patterns.

1. Warm-up review

Read these first:

Bonjour. Je m'appelle Alex. Je suis en classe lundi.

English

Hello. My name is Alex. I am in class on Monday.

2. Main reading passage

Bonjour. Je m'appelle Alex. Je suis en classe lundi avec Samira. J'ai 30 ans. J'ai une mère, un père et un frère. Samira a 28 ans. Elle a une sœur et un téléphone. Marc est notre ami. Il a dix ans dans cet exercice. Nous avons un livre en classe. Madame Dupont est la professeure. Elle a le livre. Merci, Madame Dupont. Au revoir.

4. Hidden English translation

Show full English translation

Hello. My name is Alex. I am in class on Monday with Samira. I am 30 years old. I have a mother, a father, and a brother. Samira is 28 years old. She has a sister and a phone. Marc is our friend. He is 10 years old in this exercise. We have a book in class. Madame Dupont is the teacher. She has the book. Thank you, Madame Dupont. Goodbye.

5. Comprehension questions

1. Who is in class with Alex?

Answer

Samira.

2. How old is Alex?

Answer

Alex a 30 ans.

3. What does Samira have?

Answer

Elle a une sœur et un téléphone.

4. Who has the book?

Answer

Madame Dupont a le livre.

5. What day appears in the text?

Answer

Lundi.
