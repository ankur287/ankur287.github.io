Week 2 Reading

This reading reuses Week 1 identity language and adds Week 2 avoir, age, objects, questions, and negation.

1. Warm-up review

Je suis étudiante. J'ai un livre. Nous avons la même page.

English

I am a student. I have a book. We have the same page.

2. Main reading passage

Bonjour. Je m'appelle Samira. Je suis étudiante et j'ai un livre. J'ai six ans dans cet exercice. Alex est avec moi lundi. Il a dix ans et il a un sac. Dans le sac, il a un cahier, un stylo et une clé. Marc a une carte et une question. Madame Dupont a la réponse. Nous avons la même page. Je n'ai pas de téléphone, mais Alex a un téléphone. Voilà la phrase importante.

3. Sentence-by-sentence breakdown

J'ai six ans dans cet exercice.

English

I am six years old in this exercise.

Dans le sac, il a un cahier, un stylo et une clé.

English

In the bag, he has a notebook, a pen, and a key.

Je n'ai pas de téléphone, mais Alex a un téléphone.

English

I do not have a phone, but Alex has a phone.

4. Hidden English translation

Show full English translation

Hello. My name is Samira. I am a student and I have a book. I am six years old in this exercise. Alex is with me on Monday. He is ten years old and he has a bag. In the bag, he has a notebook, a pen, and a key. Marc has a card and a question. Ms. Dupont has the answer. We have the same page. I do not have a phone, but Alex has a phone. There is the important sentence.

5. Comprehension questions

  1. Who has a book?
  2. How old is Samira in the exercise?
  3. What is in Alex's bag?
  4. Who has the answer?
  5. Who does not have a phone?
Answer
  1. Samira.
  2. Six years old.
  3. A notebook, a pen, and a key.
  4. Madame Dupont.
  5. Samira.
