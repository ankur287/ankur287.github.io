Week 9 Writing

Writing moves from controlled sentence work to a short output task using this week plus older review.

Sentence Warm-Up

  1. Copy: Je me leve a sept heures.
  2. Change the person or object: Tu te leves tot le lundi.
  3. Add one older review word: Je suis en classe avec Samira.

Guided Writing

Complete the frames with your own words.

  1. Bonjour, je m'appelle ___.
  2. Je me leve a ___.
  3. Avec Samira, je revise ___.
  4. Dans ma phrase, j'utilise aussi ___.

Controlled Writing

Use these required chunks: je me leve, tu te leves, il se leve / elle se leve, je me lave, je me prepare, je me couche. Add two review chunks: je suis, j'ai.

Writing Test

Write 10 sentences about a typical day. Use at least four reflexive verbs, three time markers, one room, one food item, and one place expression.

Checklist Before Reveal / Submit

  • I used Week 9's main grammar.
  • I reused at least three older vocabulary items.
  • I checked articles, adjective agreement, or verb form where relevant.
  • My sentences are short enough to control.
