# Grammar Map

## Main sequence
- Week 1: subject pronouns + être
- Week 2: avoir + age
- Week 3: definite/indefinite articles + gender awareness
- Week 4: adjective agreement basics
- Week 5: possessive adjectives
- Week 6: partitives + polite requests
- Week 7: question forms + vouloir / prendre basics
- Week 8: il y a + prepositions of place
- Week 9: reflexive verbs basics
- Week 10: weather and time expressions
- Week 11: present tense consolidation
- Week 12: passé composé introduction
- Week 13: imperfect + contrast with passé composé
- Week 14: necessity / obligation chunks
- Week 15: opinions and preferences
- Week 16: future forms
- Week 17: conditional for politeness
- Week 18: object pronouns, staged
- Week 19: relative clauses
- Week 20: Canadian French recognition notes
- Week 21: subjunctive awareness
- Week 22: passive voice
- Week 23: connectors and clause linking
- Week 24: formal vs informal register
- Weeks 25-30: targeted repair and review

## Teaching rules
- One main grammar target per week.
- One older grammar target must be reviewed each week.
- Use examples built from known vocabulary.
- Show patterns before exceptions.
- Do not demand free production before guided practice and later review.
- Introduce connectors gradually.
- Early weeks should prioritize comprehension over perfection.
